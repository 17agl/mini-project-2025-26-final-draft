Our Programs for Different Addictions

1. Digital Addictions (Gaming, Internet & Social Media)

Digital Detox Program
Gradual reduction in screen time with daily digital-free hours.
Mindful Technology Use
Learn to use technology consciously through guided mindfulness sessions.
Healthy Online Habits Course
Strategies to set boundaries and prioritize real-life interactions.
Focus Reboot Challenge (21 days)
Structured plan to replace gaming/social scrolling with creative or fitness activities.

2. Gambling Addiction

Financial Awareness Program
Learn money management and impulse control techniques.
Trigger Identification Workshop
Understand emotional triggers behind betting urges.
Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) Sessions
Replace risky thoughts with rational decision-making.

3. Substance Addictions (Smoking, Drinking)

QuitSmart Program
Personalized quit plans with psychological counseling.
Mind-Body Healing Program
Focus on meditation, yoga, and breathwork for withdrawal management.
Accountability Partner System
Pair with a coach or peer for daily motivation.

4. Food Addiction & Emotional Eating

Mindful Eating Program
Understand hunger vs. emotion-driven eating.
Nutrition Rebalance Course
Personalized diet planning and journaling.
Emotional Wellness Therapy
Address anxiety, guilt, or loneliness linked to overeating.

5. Work & Study Addiction

Work-Life Balance Program
Time management and relaxation techniques.
Stress & Burnout Recovery
Mindfulness and psychological therapy for chronic overworking.
Study Smart Program
Cognitive techniques to improve focus and reduce compulsive study behavior.

6. Psychological Support & Correct Guidance

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
Restructures negative thought patterns.
One-on-One Counseling
Confidential guidance from licensed therapists.
Mindfulness & Stress Management
Meditation, journaling, and breathing techniques.
Group Support Circles
Community-based peer discussions for shared healing.
Goal & Habit Coaching
Personalized plans and progress tracking.
Emotional Healing Workshops
Focus on trauma recovery and self-compassion.
